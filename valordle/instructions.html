Instructions


  1. I) Guess the VALORDLE in six tries.

  2. II) Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit.

  3. III) After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

  4. IV) If the tile turns green, the letter is in the word and in the correct spot.

  5. V) If the tile turns yellow, the letter is in the word but in the wrong position.

  6. VI) If the tile turns grey, the letter is not in the word.
